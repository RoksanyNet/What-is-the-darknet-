# About the Darknet

The darknet is a part of the internet that isn’t indexed by regular search engines. You access it using special software that helps protect your privacy.

## How to access it (step by step)

1. **Download the Tor Browser**  
   Go to the official website and download [Tor Browser](https://www.torproject.org/download/). It’s the easiest and safest way to start.

2. **Install and open it**  
   It works like a normal browser, but routes your traffic through multiple encrypted relays.

3. **Use `.onion` links**  
   Websites on the darknet usually end with `.onion`. You need the exact address to visit them.

4. **Start with trusted directories**  
   Use known directories or curated lists to find sites instead of random links. Example:
   
   [![Tor66](https://tor66.link/img/tor66_logo.png)](https://tor66.link/)

## Basic safety tips

- **Don’t share personal information**  
- **Avoid downloading unknown files**  
- **Be careful what you click**  
- **Stick to legal and safe content**  
- **Keep your browser updated**

## Important mindset

Think of the darknet like a **less regulated part of the internet**:

- It can be useful for privacy, research, or accessing censored information  
- But it also requires more caution and awareness

## Simple summary

> Install [Tor Browser](https://www.torproject.org/download/) → open `.onion` links → stay cautious and protect your privacy.
